About Me

I’m a passionate software developer with a strong foundation in mathematics. My journey into coding began during my junior year of undergraduate studies, driven by a deep curiosity about technology and problem-solving. I taught myself programming through languages like C, Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, and explored various fields including IT support and cybersecurity. Along the way, I developed a habit of continuous learning using platforms such as Coursera, Codecademy, FreeCodeCamp, Udemy, Udacity, and LeetCode.

My core interest lies in blockchain development. After discovering the transformative potential of decentralized technologies, I shifted my focus to building on Ethereum using Solidity, Hardhat, and ethers.js. I’ve worked on smart contracts, full-stack dApps, DAO mechanisms, and cryptographic protocols inspired by mathematical research like the Jacobian Conjecture. I’m committed to creating secure, ethical, and impactful blockchain systems that address real-world challenges and drive innovation in decentralized finance and governance.
